Ask me for QR code without asking
Hello everyone! As QR codes become an integral part of our daily lives, especially for digital payments, I'm working on a project called 'Ask me for QR code without asking.' The goal is to create a repository of gestures that convey specific actions, like requesting a QR code scan, without needing to say a word. These gestures can eventually become part of our everyday body language. I'd love for you to contribute to this project by recording a short video of your hand making a gesture that represents asking for a QR code.
